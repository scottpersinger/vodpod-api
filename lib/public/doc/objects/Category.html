API: Category

Videos are sorted into categories. Users who collect successful videos accrue points associated with the categories their videos belong to. Categories are a great way to discover users and videos in a particular field, and motivate users to compete to collect relevant, popular videos.

Attributes

key

The unique name for this category, e.g. "music".

name

The human name for this category, e.g. "Music".

recommended_users

An array of Users who frequently collect videos in this category.

subcategories

An array of Categorys belonging to this one.

subcategories_count

The total number of subcategories.

top_users

An array of Users, sorted from most points to least within the category.
